History of resort

Located Just Two Miles from Silver Dollar City

LTucked away in the beautiful Ozark hills in Branson, Missouri, Still Waters Resort invites you to escape the hustle and bustle of life and head to our family fun resort on the shores of Table Rock Lake, just minutes from Silver Dollar City and seven miles from the heart of Branson, Missouri.

Lakefront Condos

Still Waters Resort is located directly on Table Rock Lake just minutes from Silver Dollar City on Indian Point Road, and combines the relaxation of a peaceful lakefront escape with convenient proximity to all Branson, Missouri has to offer. Why stay at a hotel, when for the same or less price you can stay in a luxurious lakefront resort? Like Branson, Still Waters Resort has so much to offer for every age group. Whether you are wanting to book a family vacation, a group reunion, or a romantic weekend getaway, Still Waters Resort is the right choice for you!

Lakefront Condos Featuring 1 to 5 Bedroom Luxury Condos and Studio Suites ​ With our spacious condos, stylish furnishings, lake view balconies, and countless FREE amenities, Still Waters Resort is the perfect lakefront getaway. Whether you are relaxing with family, planning a business retreat, or sneaking away for a couples weekend, Still Waters Resort is the perfect destination for you and your family.
